About Sky Dreams

We're a small group of young people with a dream. As one does, we were browsing the internet, doing stuff and whatnot, when we realized, we just couldn' t find any good pictures. There's just to few places to get high-quality and royalty-free images. That's when we came together and decided, that we are going to take things into our own hands.

So we began on our quest to create the best website ever to find images. And that's exactly what we did. Our great Website features dozens of high-quality royalty-free pictures, which you can all download and use. But we're not done yet! We are striving to improve our website and add more pictures and you can greatly help to achieve this by donating!
